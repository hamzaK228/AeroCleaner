Consequences of Smog

air pollution effects.

Smog does not just look bad—it causes a cascade of biological, ecological, and economic emergencies that threaten the future of our metropolitan centers.

🫁

Severe Health Risks

Causes chronic breathing issues, asthma flares, lung cancer, and cardiovascular diseases, heavily impacting children and the elderly.

🌍

Accelerating Climate

Suspended carbon aerosol particles absorb solar radiation, exacerbating localized urban heat islands and global climate anomalies.

🌿

Ecosystem Damage

Particulates settle on leaves, blocking photosynthesis, reducing soil fertility, and poisoning vital plant and animal habitats.

🌧️

Acid Precipitation

Sulfur and nitrogen oxides dissolve into rain, damaging forests, acidifying water supplies, and corroding urban structures.

👁️

Reduced Visibility

Dense aerosol particles scatter light, drastically reducing street visibility, which causes traffic delays and increases serious accidents.

💸

Economic Drain

Leads to billions in excess healthcare expenditure, massive crop yield reductions, and decreased workforce labor productivity.
